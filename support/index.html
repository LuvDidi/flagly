How do I sync my progress across devices?

Open Settings inside Flagly and enable iCloud Sync. Your flag progress and achievements will sync automatically across all devices signed into the same Apple ID.

How can I reset my progress?

Go to Settings → Reset Progress. Long-press the button for 2 seconds to confirm. This action is permanent.

Why is my progress different between devices?

Make sure iCloud Sync is enabled on every device and that they're all signed into the same Apple ID. Initial sync after enabling can take a few minutes.

How is difficulty determined?

Flagly tracks your mastery level for each flag individually. Three modes — Discovery, Training, Mastery — unlock progressively as you get more flags right.

Does Flagly collect personal data?

No. Flagly works fully offline. Your quiz progress is stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your private iCloud account. We don't have servers, we don't collect analytics, we don't track you. Read the full Privacy Policy.

I have a feature request or found a bug

Email us at contact.flagly@gmail.com with as much detail as possible (device model, iOS version, steps to reproduce). Screenshots are welcome.
