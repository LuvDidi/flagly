Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 31, 2026

This policy describes how Flagly handles your data.

Data We Collect

Flagly does not collect any personal information. The app does not require an account, and we have no access to your identity.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your flag learning progress and unlocked achievements are stored in your private iCloud container. This data is tied to your Apple ID and managed entirely by Apple. We cannot access, read, or share this data.

Local Data

Quiz sessions, app preferences (theme, notification settings), and other usage data are stored locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to any server.

Notifications

Flagly can send optional practice reminders. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device. No notification data is sent to external servers.

Third-Party Services

Flagly does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks. There are no third-party SDKs in the app.

Children's Privacy

Flagly does not collect personal information from anyone, including children. The app is suitable for all ages.

Data Retention

Local data is deleted when you uninstall the app. iCloud data can be managed through your device's iCloud settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: contact.flagly@gmail.com
